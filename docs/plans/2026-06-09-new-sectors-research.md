# New Career Sectors from DSD + Minor Combinations — Research

**Date:** 2026-06-09
**Task:** audio-ce4 (follow-up to the Suggested-Minors design)
**Status:** Research deliverable

## Purpose

The [Suggested-Minors design](2026-06-09-suggested-minors-design.md) showed that
a Digital Sound Design (DSD) major **plus a well-chosen DSU minor** opens audio
career fields the major alone doesn't cover. This document does the follow-up:
for each of those expanded sectors, propose **real, hiring employers** so the
directory has actual entries in them rather than empty sector options.

The sector options below already exist in `Employers → Sector`. What's missing is
employers tagged into them. Everything here is a **candidate** — it still goes
through Tate's `Last Verified` gate before it appears on the site.

## How to read each entry

- **Unlocks via** — the DSD + minor combination that prepares a student for it.
- **Why** — one line on the work.
- **Sample employers** — real organizations that hire audio/sound people in that
  space. Mix of makers, studios, labs, and end-users so there's a range of size
  and geography. Career-page verification still needed per the usual workflow.

---

## 1. AV Systems Integration
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Network & Security Administration / Project Management
- **Why:** Design, install, and commission sound + AV for corporate campuses,
  stadiums, houses of worship, courtrooms, and control rooms.
- **Sample employers:** AVI-SPL · Diversified · Solotech · Ford Audio-Video ·
  CCS Presentation Systems · Crestron (manufacturer) · Biamp (manufacturer)

## 2. Automotive & Embedded Audio
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Computer Science / Applied Mathematics
- **Why:** In-cabin sound systems, tuning, active noise control, and embedded
  audio DSP for vehicles and devices.
- **Sample employers:** Harman International (JBL/Harman Kardon automotive) ·
  Bose Automotive · Cerence · Bang & Olufsen Automotive · Cirrus Logic ·
  Analog Devices · Texas Instruments (audio DSP)

## 3. Generative & AI Audio
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Computer Science / AI & Machine Learning *(ambitious)*
- **Why:** Build and evaluate models that generate music, sound effects, and
  audio; dataset + model-quality work needs trained ears.
- **Sample employers:** Suno · Udio · Stability AI (Stable Audio) ·
  Meta AI (AudioCraft/MusicGen) · Google DeepMind (audio) · LANDR (AI mastering) ·
  Aimi

## 4. Voice & Conversational AI
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Computer Science / AI & Machine Learning *(ambitious)*
- **Why:** Text-to-speech, voice cloning, speech enhancement, and voice-assistant
  audio quality.
- **Sample employers:** ElevenLabs · Cerence · SoundHound · Respeecher ·
  WellSaid Labs · Deepgram · Speechmatics

## 5. Acoustics Consulting
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Physics / Applied Mathematics
- **Why:** Architectural and environmental acoustics — concert halls, offices,
  noise studies, room design.
- **Sample employers:** Arup Acoustics · Acentech · Threshold Acoustics ·
  Jaffe Holden · Kirkegaard Associates · Veneklasen Associates ·
  Charles M. Salter Associates

## 6. Audio Test & Measurement
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Physics / Applied Mathematics
- **Why:** Build and run the gear and software that measures audio quality;
  device QA and validation.
- **Sample employers:** Audio Precision · NTi Audio · Brüel & Kjær (HBK) ·
  Listen Inc. (SoundCheck) · Klippel · GRAS Sound & Vibration

## 7. Spatial & Immersive Audio
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Physics / Computer Science
- **Why:** Object-based and 3D audio — formats, renderers, and tools for film,
  music, and devices.
- **Sample employers:** Dolby Laboratories (Atmos) · DTS / Xperi ·
  Sony (360 Reality Audio) · Sennheiser (AMBEO) · L-Acoustics (L-ISA) ·
  Steinberg

## 8. Hearing Health & Audiology
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Physics / Biology / Computer Science
- **Why:** Hearing aids, cochlear implants, and "hearables" — signal processing
  and product audio for hearing health.
- **Sample employers:** Starkey · Sonova (Phonak) · GN (ReSound/Jabra) ·
  WS Audiology (Widex/Signia) · Demant (Oticon) · Cochlear · Whisper.ai

## 9. Bioacoustics & Soundscape Ecology
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Biology (Ecology/Conservation) / Computer Science
- **Why:** Record, classify, and monitor wildlife and environmental sound for
  conservation and research.
- **Sample employers:** Cornell Lab of Ornithology (K. Lisa Yang Center for
  Conservation Bioacoustics) · Wildlife Acoustics · Rainforest Connection ·
  Conservation Metrics · NOAA Fisheries (marine bioacoustics)

## 10. Forensic Audio
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Digital Forensics *(computer forensics — adjacent)* /
  Project Management
- **Why:** Audio evidence enhancement, authentication, and analysis for legal
  and investigative work. (Many roles are with labs or law enforcement.)
- **Sample employers:** Primeau Forensics · Cognitech · Owl Investigations ·
  National Center for Media Forensics (CU Denver) · FBI/DHS forensic labs ·
  iZotope (RX — tooling side)

## 11. Localization & Dubbing
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Spanish / English for New Media
- **Why:** Audio side of localizing games, film, and TV — recording, editing,
  and mixing dubbed dialogue across languages.
- **Sample employers:** Keywords Studios · Iyuno · VSI · Formosa Group ·
  Deluxe (Sfera) · Side (PTW)

## 12. Accessibility & Captioning
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + English for New Media / Communication Studies
- **Why:** Audio description, captioning, and described media — making content
  usable for blind/low-vision and deaf/hard-of-hearing audiences.
- **Sample employers:** 3Play Media · Verbit · Rev · Vitac (CaptionMax) ·
  Descript (tooling) · Described and Captioned Media Program (DCMP)

## 13. Audiobooks & Spoken-Word
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + English / Communication Studies / Digital Content Creation
- **Why:** Record, edit, and master audiobooks and spoken-word; growing,
  remote-friendly field.
- **Sample employers:** Audible (Amazon) · Penguin Random House Audio ·
  Macmillan Audio · RBmedia (Recorded Books) · Findaway Voices (Spotify) ·
  Deyan Audio

## 14. EdTech & Learning Media
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Video Production / Digital Content Creation
- **Why:** Audio for e-learning, language apps, and educational media —
  narration, sound design, and capture.
- **Sample employers:** Duolingo · Khan Academy · Coursera · Pearson ·
  McGraw Hill · Babbel

## 15. Audio Preservation & Archives
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + History / Digital Content Creation *(archival/digitization
  skills)*
- **Why:** Digitize, restore, and preserve historical recordings for libraries,
  museums, and archives.
- **Sample employers:** Library of Congress (National Audio-Visual Conservation
  Center) · Smithsonian Folkways · Northeast Document Conservation Center (NEDCC) ·
  George Blood LP · The Internet Archive · National Archives (NARA)

## 16. Simulation & XR Training
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Computer Science
- **Why:** Audio for training simulators (defense, aviation, medical) and
  enterprise XR training — realism and spatialization matter.
- **Sample employers:** CAE · Lockheed Martin (training & simulation) ·
  L3Harris · Strivr · Talespin · Gaumard Scientific (medical sim)

## 17. Casino & Slot Game Audio
- **Unlocks via:** DSD + Computer Science
- **Why:** Sound design, music, and audio implementation for slot machines and
  casino games — a steady, often-overlooked game-audio niche.
- **Sample employers:** Light & Wonder (formerly Scientific Games) · Aristocrat ·
  IGT (International Game Technology) · Konami Gaming · Everi · AGS

---

## Recommendations

1. **The sectors are worth keeping** — each maps to a real, hiring field and a
   concrete DSD + minor path. No new sector options need to be invented; the set
   already in Airtable is good.
2. **Populate, don't just label.** Several of these sectors currently have few or
   no employers. The lists above are the candidates to add so each sector has
   real entries.
3. **Verification workflow unchanged.** Add as drafts, confirm the careers page,
   set `Last Verified`, and they appear on the site automatically on the next
   build.
4. **Highest-leverage to seed first** (broad hiring + clear DSD fit):
   Spatial & Immersive Audio, Generative & AI Audio, Voice & Conversational AI,
   Automotive & Embedded Audio, Audiobooks & Spoken-Word.
